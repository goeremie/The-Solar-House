The Solar House is a retail organization that helps in the promotion of harnessing solar power energy from the sun which is then accordingly converted into thermal or electrical energy. Solar energy nowadays is seen as one of the cleanest and most abundant renewable energy source available and we're here to help in spreading its usage.

The solar technologies we sell will harness this energy for a variety of uses, including generating electricity, providing light or a comfortable interior environment, and heating water for domestic, commercial, or industrial use.
